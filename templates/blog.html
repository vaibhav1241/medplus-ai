Building a Symptom-Based Diagnosis Web App

In the age of technology, access to timely healthcare is more critical than ever. We developed MedPulse AI to help users identify potential illnesses based on their symptoms using Flask and Machine Learning.

The Solution

Our web app allows users to enter symptoms and uses a pre-trained model to predict the most likely disease instantly. It provides comprehensive info like descriptions, precautions, medications, and diets.

Key Features

  • Intelligent Prediction: Uses advanced ML models for accuracy.
  • Comprehensive Advice: Includes precautions, diets, and workouts.
  • Modern Interface: Designed with a minimalist clinical aesthetic.
